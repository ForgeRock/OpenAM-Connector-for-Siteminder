Libraries needed for building the SiteMinder connector

*** For OpenAM 9.5 ***
Library name (source)
----------------------------------
amserver.jar (OpenAM webapp)
commons-codec-1.3.jar (OpenAM webapp)
openfedlib.jar (OpenAM webapp)
opensso-sharedlib.jar (OpenAM webapp)
servlet-api.jar (Tomcat)
smjavaagentapi.jar (SiteMinder WebAgent/SDK)
SmJavaApi.jar (SiteMinder SDK)

*** For OpenAM 11.0 ***
Library name (source)
----------------------------------
commons-codec-1.6.jar (OpenAM webapp)
openam-core-11.0.0.jar (OpenAM webapp)
openam-federation-library-11.0.0.jar (OpenAM webapp)
openam-shared-11.0.0.jar (OpenAM webapp)
servlet-api.jar (Tomcat)
smjavaagentapi.jar (SiteMinder WebAgent/SDK)
SmJavaApi.jar (SiteMinder SDK)
